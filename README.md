# Business_Analytics_Project_BAN644
Customer Churn Prediction- Predict which customers are likely to stop using a service.
Project Overview: Customer Churn Prediction (The "Telecom Retention" Initiative)
The Problem: Customer acquisition is significantly more expensive than customer retention. The business is experiencing a high volume of customers canceling their telecommunications and internet services. The retention team needs a proactive, data-driven way to identify which customers are at the highest risk of leaving (churning) so they can intervene with targeted promotions, discounts, or service upgrades before the cancellation happens.

The Objective: 1. Build a robust machine learning classification model to predict the binary target variable (Churn).
2. Identify the core drivers of customer turnover (e.g., analyzing if "Month-to-month contracts," "Electronic check payments," or a "lack of tech support" are pushing customers away).

The Methodology:

Data Source: Telecom Customer Churn Dataset (churn_Data.csv). https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blastchar/telco-customer-churn/discussion?sort=hotness

Machine Learning Algorithms: * Logistic Regression (Provides a strong, highly interpretable baseline to easily explain why a customer is leaving to business stakeholders).

Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) / Deep Learning (Utilized for advanced feature pattern recognition and to push predictive accuracy to its absolute limit).
